Selene: Goddess of the Moon

About Selene:

In Greek mythology, Selene held a vital role as the personification of the moon. As a Titan goddess and the daughter of Hyperion and Theia, she was an essential figure in the celestial hierarchy, illuminating the night sky and governing the lunar cycle. Source.
